OUR LATEST EVENTS

Rotary is a global network of 1.2 million neighbours, friends, leaders, and problem-solvers who see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change - across the globe, in our communities, and in ourselves.

July 04, 2023

Eye Checkup Camp

A total of 395 students had their eyes checked, 54 of whom needed refractive glasses and 12 were referred for cyclo refraction, 5 others were referred to a clinic for further intervention. In addition, 14 staff members also had their eyes checked.

July 13, 2023

Installation of interact club at jagruti school for blind girls

Jagruti School for Blind Girls has been set up by the National Foundation for Blind - Maharashtra. A number of Rotary Clubs of Pune got together and aided in construction of this school's new building. RC Pune Central has formed an Interact Club there in 1995 and has been supporting it since then.

July 07, 2023

Tree Plantation At Sunrise Green Hills Kharadi

Tree Plantation Drive was Organized by Sunrise Green Hills Foundation in Association with Rotary Club of Pune Central and Inner Wheel Club of Pune Central.
